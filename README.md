## Vision Statement

Providing a free budgeting service to help people manage their finances.

## Motivation

The goal of this project is to build a web application that helps users track income and expenses, organize spending by category, and stay within monthly budgets.

Users will be able to log transactions as they happen and view a dashboard summarizing spending trends and budget status.

## Risks to Project Completion

* Asynchronous workflow and scheduling limitations
* Most team members have no prior experience with Agile workflows
* Scope creep and time constraints due to the compressed semester schedule

## Risk Mitigation Strategy

* Schedule weekly meetings during the team’s agreed availability timeslot
* Complete weekly check-ins through email and Zoom meetings
* Keep the project within scope by limiting extra features

## Development Method

The team will follow an Agile/Scrum approach with weekly reviews focused on the following questions:

* What did you do last week?
* What will you do this week?
* Are there any blockers or impediments?

The project will be broken into smaller tasks with regular testing throughout development.
